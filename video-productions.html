Video Productions

A selection of narrative shortfilms and documentary-style projects.

Mysterious Case of Daniella Hale (2022):

A capstone project to illustrate excellecence in digital filmmaking and communication to audience through multimedia.

Whispered Promises (2022):

A class project for music video concept. The story follows a person during the inner conflicts of depression. This style uses dark contrasts and greyscale to illustrate the moody tone. By the end the person finds hope in the people around them.

Buck Brother (2021):

As part of my TV production class, we were required to create a mini TV series. I took one of my favorite shows Big Brother and make a mockumentary on it set in Appalachia. Because the class only required four episodes, it naturally leaves off with a cliffhanger.

Diane (2020):

A Tragic fantasy where a dead woman, Diane, from the 50's falls in love with a current day man. She must do what it takes to win the heart of a man who doesn't love her back.

Christian Misconception (2020):

This video is a documentary-style interview with Christians breaking down the sterotypes of Christianity.

1,000 Words, Two Years (2020):

A lot can happen in Two years. In our ironic "speaking out" silent film project we had to demonstrate a narrative with only imagery.
